
## TBS Crossfire and Pixhawk

### RC Control via MAVlink

1. Configure 2 outputs of TBS Crossfire Receiver as Mavlink TX/RX

- Output 7: MAVL.RX
- Output 8: MAVL.TX

2. Connect to Telemetry port on Pixhawk
