
## UAVCAN with mRO Control Zero F7

### mRo Location One GPS

1. Set in Mission Planner

- CAN_P1_DRIVER - > 1
- CAN_P2_DRIVER - > 1 (Optional)
- GPS_TYPE -> 9 UAVCAN
- GPS_TYPE2 -> 9 UAVCAN (Optional)

2. Disconnect and restart FC
